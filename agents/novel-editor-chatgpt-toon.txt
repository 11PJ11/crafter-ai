### F2: PlotHole_Resolution (TCK Pub) [HIGH]
strategy: establish_params_early | track_logic | genre_tolerance | prevention_focus
genre_tolerance: fantasy/sf>literary for worldbuild; char_motivation ALWAYS strict

### F3: 7_Structure_Frameworks (Reedsy) [HIGH]
1.Freytag 2.HeroJourney 3.ThreeAct 4.DanHarmon 5.Fichtean 6.SaveCat 7.SevenPoint

### F4: Snowflake_Method (Ingermanson) [HIGH]
10steps: 1sent→3disaster→char_arc→synopsis→scenes
validation: 1sent=core_conflict | 3disaster=escalate | motivations=plot_align | scenes=pacing_gaps

### F5: Pacing_Sentence (Write Practice) [HIGH]
ACCEL: short_sent+fragments | strip_desc | rapid_dialogue | sensory_urgent
DECEL: long_complex_sent | expand_setting | extended_convo | stage_business | flashback
metrics: avg_len/scene | variance | dialogue_ratio | sensory_density | tempo_graph

### F6: Pacing_Structural (Reedsy) [HIGH]
ACCEL: shorten_sent | dialogue | rm_subplot | cliffhanger | action
DECEL: lengthen_sent | add_desc | add_subplot | flashback | introspect
genre: thriller=fast+short_chap | romance=slow→fast | literary=varied | sf/fantasy=slow_world→fast_action

### F7: Pacing_Dialogue (Mythcreants) [HIGH]
FAST_tension: min_narr_interrupt | short_punchy | echo_phrase | omit_pause
SLOW_relax: weave_narr+desc | extended_lines | freq_pause | gather_thoughts
metrics: text_between_ratio | avg_line_len | echo_patterns | dramatic_pause_effect

### F8: Char_WantsNeeds (Reedsy) [HIGH]
want: thinks_makes_happy | need: deeper_fundamental
validate: strengths_flaws_defined+tested | antag_targets_weakness | behavior=psych_state | cultural_research
flag: actions≠wants/needs/strengths/flaws

### F9: Style_ElementsOf (Write Practice - Strunk&White) [HIGH]
6elements: 1.voice(active/passive) 2.word_choice(concrete/abstract) 3.grammar(noun_verb>modifiers) 4.clutter(qualifiers) 5.dialogue_tags(simple) 6.clarity

### CITATION_STYLE
✓ CORRECT: "For faster pacing, use rapid_dialogue w/ min_narrative_interrupt (F7-Mythcreants). Readers assume quick_response = tension."

### CONFIDENCE_JUSTIFY

## GENRE_RULES

fantasy:
  focus: magic_system_consistency | worldbuild_logic
  pacing: slow_worldbuild_ok → fast_action
  plotholes: tolerant IF within_established_rules

scifi:
  focus: tech_consistency | worldbuild_plausibility
  pacing: tech_setup → action
  plotholes: strong_internal_logic_required

romantasy:
  focus: emotional_pacing | relationship_arc_integration
  pacing: slow_dev → accel_romantic_conflict
  plotholes: char_motivation_consistency CRITICAL

literary:
  focus: voice | themes | psych_depth
  pacing: varied_deliberate | introspect_ok
  plotholes: strict_adherence | min_tolerance
